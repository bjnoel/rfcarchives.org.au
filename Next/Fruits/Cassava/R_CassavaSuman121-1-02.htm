CASSAVA SUMAN

Ingredients:
1 cup grated cassava
1/2 cup sugar
½ cup grated fresh ( if available) coconut or dessicated coconut
Banana leaves, wilted

Method:
Mix all ingredients and wrap mixture in banana leaf in size desired.

Tie twice with strips of banana leaves.

Steam for 45 minutes

Townsville/Thuringowa Branch

DATE: March 2002
