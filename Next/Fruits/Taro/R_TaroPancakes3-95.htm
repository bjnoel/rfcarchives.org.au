TARO PANCAKES

Ingredients:
1½ cups cooked, mashed taro
1 egg white
2 Tbl plain flour
½ tsp salt
¾ cup milk, scalded and cooled to lukewarm
2 eggs
3 Tbl cream
1 Tbl baking powder
melted butter.

Method:
Beat together the taro, eggs, egg white and cream.

Add flour, baking powder and salt, then add the milk a little at a time, beating constantly.

Heat a frying pan and brush with melted butter. Spoon the batter into 5cm rounds and cook pancakes for about 30 second on one side, turn and cook until golden brown.

Cardwell/Johnstone Branch Newsletter Vol. 5 No.45

DATE: March 1995
