RARE FRUIT HEALTH CAKE

Ingredients:
ANY FRUIT MAY BE USED FOR THIS RECIPE
1 cup soursop pulp
2 very ripe yellow sapote
1 cup pure apple juice
½ cup sultanas
½ cup cold pressed oil
1 Tbl (or more) chopped ginger
1 Tbl (or more) lemon rind
2 eggs
3 cups Whole Meal Self-Raising flour

Method:
Blend together all fruit ingredients and oil, then add eggs, then flour, mix well. Pour into greased tin, and bake 1 hour in a moderate oven.
