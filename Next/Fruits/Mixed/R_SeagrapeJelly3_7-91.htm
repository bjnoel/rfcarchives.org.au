SEAGRAPE JELLY III (Using pectin)

Ingredients:
4 cups seagrape juice
5 Tbl lemon or lime juice
5 cups sugar
1 box powdered pectin

Method:
Place prepared juice in large pan (6-8qt). Turn to highest heat. Add lemon juice and entire contents of box of powdered pectin. Stir to dissolve and continue stirring until mixture comes to a hard boil.

Add sugar. Boil, stirring constantly until mixture sheets off the spoon or reaches 224°F on a jelly thermometer. Pour into hot, sterilized glasses and seal at once.

Capricornia Branch News Vol.8 No.3

DATE: July 1991
