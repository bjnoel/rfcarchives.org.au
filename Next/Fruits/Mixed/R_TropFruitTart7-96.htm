TROPICAL FRUIT CONDENSED MILK TART

Method:
1. Crush one pack of biscuits.

2. Add melted copha.

3. Spray tin with cooking spray.

4. Line cake tin with biscuit mix.

5. Put in fridge to set.

6. Mix condensed milk and lime juice.

7. Put in biscuit base.

8. Place in fridge to set.

9. When set, decorate with tropical fruit.

Winning entry by Dion Schloss aged 13,
Capricornia Branch Newsletter Vol. 13 No.3

DATE: July 1996
