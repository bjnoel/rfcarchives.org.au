GLACE FRUITS

Ingredients:
Cumquats, pineapple, jujube

Syrup:
½ cup sugar
½ cup water
1 Tbl lemon juice.

Method:
These fruits are made by a simple process. Put fruit into a saucepan with cold water. Bring to the boil, empty out boiled water then repeat twice more and drain.

Mix the syrup ingredients and bring to boil. Boil 5 minutes. Cool.

When cold, add fruit and syrup together.

These are lovely for the Christmas table especially when displayed in pretty patty cake papers.

Jose, Townsville

DATE: September 1983
