UVILLA MUFFINS *

Ingredients:
1½ cups S.R. Flour
¼ tsp nutmeg
¼ tsp mixed spice
½ cup brown sugar
½ cup sultanas
¼ cup vegetable oil
1 egg
½ cup Uvilla Puree
½ cup milk.

Method:
To make Uvilla Puree - de-seed Uvillas and place whole fruit in blender. Force through cheese cloth or similar.

Sift dry ingredients, add sugar and sultanas. Beat egg, add oil, fruit, milk and blend thoroughly. Add to dry ingredients - stir well till mixed. Put in deep patty tins - cook in moderate oven 18 - 20 minutes.

* Editor's note: uvilla is also called Amazon Tree Grape.

Mrs. Eileen Aldridge

DATE: February 1984
