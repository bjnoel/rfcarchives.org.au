JUJUBE GRAHAM BREAD

Ingredients:
3 cups graham flour (or whole wheat flour)
2 cups jujubes, cut in small pieces
1 cup white flour
1½ tsp baking powder
1 tsp soda bicarb
1 tsp salt
½ cup sugar
2 cups thick sour milk
1 egg

Method:
Bake one hour in a not-too-hot oven.

Roger Meyer,
Tropical Fruit News Vo1.24 No.4 April, 1990

DATE: March 1992
