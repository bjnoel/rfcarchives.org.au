JUJUBE CAKE

Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
½ cup of lard (not melted)
2 cups dried jujubes, diced
2 cups wheat flour
1 tsp soda bicarb
½ tsp salt

Method:
Bring lard, sugar and jujubes to a boil in a pot, then set aside to cool.

Mix dry ingredients together, then mix all together.

Bake in a moderate oven.

Roger Meyer,
Tropical Fruit News Vo1.24 No.4 April, 1990

DATE: March 1992
