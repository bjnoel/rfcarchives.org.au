MAMEY SAPOTE PIE II

Ingredients:
3 large eggs
½ cup plain flour
½ cup sugar
½ cup coconut
2 Tbl butter
1½ cups milk
1 cup mashed Mamey sapote
½ tsp cinnamon

Method:
Grease 23cm pie plate and dust with flour. Place all ingredients in processor or blender and process until well-blended.

Pour into pie plate and bake in moderate oven for 1 hour or until custard is set.

Jane Borg,
Port Curtis Branch

DATE: September 1994
