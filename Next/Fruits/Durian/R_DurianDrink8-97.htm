DURIAN DRINK

Ingredients:
durian
condensed milk
sugar or syrup
ice

Method:
Easy. Peel durian meat into a blender. Blend it for a short while.

Pour the durian puree into a glass of condensed milk - amount of condensed milk to taste.

Stir it up and chill it or add ice to drink.

If preferred, add syrup manis [sweet syrup - DOL] or rose syrup to taste and for appearance.

Enjoy it? But don't keep the drink too long or overnight.

DATE: August 1997
