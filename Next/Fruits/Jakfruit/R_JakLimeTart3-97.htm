JAKFRUIT LIME TART

Ingredients:
tart shell, cooked
1 tin condensed milk
1½ cups pureed jakfruit
juice of 2 limes
4 tsp gelatine dissolved in small amount of hot water.

Method:
Combine all ingredients thoroughly. Fill tart shell. Decorate. Chill to set.

Solitaire Schloss

DATE: March 1997
