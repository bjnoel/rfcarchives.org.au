BATTERED JAKFRUIT FRITTERS

Ingredients:
3 Tbl plain flour
1/3 cup corn flour
1 Tbl baking powder (optional)
½ cup milk
1 egg
ripe, prepared pieces of jakfruit

Method:
Mix batter and dip ripe pieces of jakfruit (deseeded). Fry until golden brown, preferably in safflower oil.

Debby Shah
Darwin Branch News July '87

DATE: January 1988
