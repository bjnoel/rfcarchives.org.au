JAKFRUIT JAM

Ingredients:
ripe but firm jakfruit
lemon juice
sugar

Method:
Prepare jakfruit pulp and slice finely. For each cup of pulp, add a cup of sugar. For each 2 cups of pulp and 2 cups of sugar, add the juice of one or two lemons.

Place all in saucepan, mix well, cook until mixture thickens. Bottle in sterilized jars.

Joan Crees
Mossman Digest No.14, May, 1989

DATE: March 1990
