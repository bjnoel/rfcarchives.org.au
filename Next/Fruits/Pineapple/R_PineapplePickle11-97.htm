PINEAPPLE MUSTARD PICKLE

Ingredients:
½ cup pineapple, diced
450 g sugar
80 ml white wine vinegar
25 g dry mustard
125 ml water

Method:
Combine the sugar and water and cook until the sugar is completely dissolved.

Add the fruit and cook over moderate heat for 15 minutes.

Remove from heat and put into a bowl.

Simmer the vinegar over low heat and stir in the mustard powder.

Cool, and then add it to the fruit mixture.

Roy Vogelpoel,
Extract from The Art Of Preserving Fruit

DATE: August 1997
