JAMAICAN DREAM

Ingredients:
200 g pineapple flesh
100 g bananas
125 ml fresh coconut milk
2 Tbl fresh grated coconut

Method:
Cut and put pineapple through a juicer.

Peel and chop the bananas.

Blend the banana, juice, coconut milk and grated coconut to a creamy consistency.

DATE: November 1995
