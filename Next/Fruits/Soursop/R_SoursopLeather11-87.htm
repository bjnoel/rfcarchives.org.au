SOURSOP SUNDAE FRUIT LEATHER

Ingredients:
1 medium soursop
½ red apple
½ cup pecan or hazel nuts
1 medium ripe banana
½ small pawpaw

Method:
Peel and de-seed all fruit, blend together, spread on plastic-wrapped trays and dry in food dehydrator 10 to 12 hours.

Dawn Meneikys, Mossman Digest August, 1985

DATE: November 1987
