GUAVA HONEY RECIPE

Method:
Slice guavas, add a small amount of water and simmer until thick (about ½ original bulk).

Add honey to taste, towards the end.

Push through sieve to de-seed.

Spread evenly in flat non-porous tray or on foil to thickness of about ⅛ in, (3mm).

Dry in the sun for 2 to 3 days, or in dryer.

Turn once and slice into strips.

Lauren Gartrell

DATE: May 1983
