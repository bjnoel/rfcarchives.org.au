STAR APPLE SALAD

Ingredients:
wedges of star apple (no seeds)
julienne smoked chicken (thin strips)
rockmelon, diced
lettuce leaves
yogurt with chopped mint leaves for dressing, or grain mustard plus mayonnaise

Method:
Toss rockmelon, chicken and star apple together.

Place the mixture in lettuce leaf cups and add dressing.

Jenny Kirk

DATE: March 1997
