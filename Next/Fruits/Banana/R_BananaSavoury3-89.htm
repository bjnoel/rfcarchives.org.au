BANANA SAVOURY

Ingredients:
2 bananas, not too ripe
salt, cayenne
beaten egg
fine breadcrumbs

Method:
Peel bananas. Cut each banana into small lengths. Season with salt and a little cayenne, then dip each piece in beaten egg and roll in fine breadcrumbs.

Fry till golden brown in hot butter. Serve hot on small fingers of toast.

Ingham Newsletter April 1987

DATE: March 1989
