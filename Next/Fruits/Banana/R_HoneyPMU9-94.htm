HONEY PICK ME UP

Ingredients:
2 cups well-chilled milk
2 eggs
¼ cup honey
1 large banana
2 nips Galliano (optional)
Freshly grated nutmeg

Method:
Place milk, honey, eggs, banana and Galliano in a blender, blend until mixture is smooth.

Serve in tall chilled goblets. Top with grated nutmeg.

N.B. Without the Galliano, this recipe is a nourishing drink for two people. With it, it serves six cocktails.

Serve with fresh cream or icecream.

Mossman Digest No.33 September 1994

DATE: July 1994
