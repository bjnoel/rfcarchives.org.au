BANANA OATIES

Ingredients:
185 gm butter or margarine
1 cup sugar
1 egg
1 cup well-mashed bananas
1 cup rolled oats
½ cup chopped nuts
1½ cups plain flour
½ tsp bi-carb. soda
¼ tsp nutmeg
¾ tsp cinnamon

Method:
Beat together butter and sugar until creamy; add the egg and beat well. Mix in the bananas, oats and nuts. Sift together flour, soda, and spices, add and mix in thoroughly. Drop teaspoons of mixture onto greased oven trays and bake in moderately hot oven 12 - 15 minutes.

DATE: January 1984
