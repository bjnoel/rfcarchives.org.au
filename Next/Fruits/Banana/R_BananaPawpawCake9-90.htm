BANANA AND PAWPAW CAKE

Ingredients:
1½ cups SR flour
1 cup chopped pawpaw
4 oz. low fat cottage cheese
4 egg whites
2 large mashed bananas
2 Tbl honey
1 tsp vanilla

Method:
Blend cottage cheese and pawpaw until smooth, add one egg white at a time, add vanilla and banana, and fold in sifted flour.

Gently spoon mixture into an 8" cake tin. Bake at 350ºF or 175ºC for 45 minutes.

Margaret Hastie
Mossman Digest Nov. No.16

DATE: September 1990
