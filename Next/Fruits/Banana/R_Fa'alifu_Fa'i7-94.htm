FA'ALIFU FA'I

Ingredients:
mature firm green plantains
chopped onion
salt and pepper
coconut cream
curry powder

Method:
Peel plantains. Cut into large sections and boil in salted water for about 20-30 minutes until cooked. After boiling, discard the water.

Add coconut cream, onion, salt, pepper and curry powder and simmer for 5-10 minutes before serving.

DATE: July 1994
