COMMENTS
ARKIN was the most mature fruit sample almost to the point of being overripe.
FUANG TUNG, B16 and the seedling were the least mature, but still mature enough for harvest.

ARKIN had a dimpled skin that harboured sooty mould.
B10 had a very smooth skin, wax-like in appearance.
The seedling had a skin that was also smooth.
GIANT SIAM and B10 had the thinnest skin with Giant Siam showing handling marks. FUANG TUNG was popular due to its large size and attractive wavy wings.

Thanks to the Fitzroy Nursery for supplying most of the fruit.

Roger Goebel
