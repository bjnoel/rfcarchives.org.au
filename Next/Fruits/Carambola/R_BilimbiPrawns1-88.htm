BILIMBI PRAWNS

Ingredients:
12 shelled and de-veined prawns
1 large onion
1 clove of garlic crushed
1 Tbl chilli powder (or less according to taste)
½ tsp turmeric
14 bilimbis sliced
1 Tbl oil

Method:
Heat oil, fry garlic and onion, until golden, add turmeric, chilli powder, and a little water, fry for one minute, add prawns, then when prawns are cooked, add bilimbi and salt to taste. Cook another five minutes and serve.

Gwen Henderson,
Darwin Branch News

DATE: January 1988
