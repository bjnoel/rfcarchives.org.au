STAR OF DAVID PUNCH

Ingredients:
6 carambolas sliced
2 cups strong tea with 24 cloves
½ cup sugar or honey
½ cup lime juice
2 cups cranberry or rosella juice
2 cups orange juice
gingerale

Method:
Slice carambolas and put on cookie sheet and freeze until ready to serve.

Mix other ingredients in punch bowl. Add ginger ale and ice. Float carambola slices on top.

Living off the Land News Vol.16 No.5

DATE: May 1991
