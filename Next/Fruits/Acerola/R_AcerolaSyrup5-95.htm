ACEROLA OR BARBADOS CHERRY IN SYRUP

Method:
Remove stems from fruit, cover fruit with sugar (about 1½ cups of sugar to a hatful of berries).

Put in microwave on high for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally, or put on stove and slowly bring to boil, cook until fruit is soft and pulpy.

B. Lowe
Capricornia Branch News Vol.11 No.5

DATE: May 1995
