BARBADOS CHERRIES IN BRANDY

Ingredients:
1 kg cherries
500 g sugar
2 cups of water
brandy
1 piece of cinnamon

Method:
Remove the stems and wash the cherries. Boil cherries, water and sugar for 2 minutes. Open lid only after the contents has cooled, otherwise the cherries will wrinkle. Mix brandy with the fruit and juice to taste, and store in jars.

Mildred Kappel, Townsville Branch

DATE: November 1983
