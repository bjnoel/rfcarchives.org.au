BLUEBARB JAM

Ingredients:
3 cups finely cut rhubarb
7 cups sugar
3 cups crushed blueberries
1 cup liquid fruit pectin

Method:
Combine rhubarb and blueberries in a large saucepan, add sugar and mix. Place over high heat and bring to full boil for one minute. Stir constantly.

Ladle into hot sterilized jars and seal.

DATE: January 1985
