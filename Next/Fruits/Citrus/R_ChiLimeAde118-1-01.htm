CHICAGO LIMEADE

Ingredients:
1½ Tbl simple syrup
1 lime (cut into three - two large ends, one middle "wheel")
1 oz soda water
1 oz 7-up

Method:
Squeeze the two lime ends. Fill a mason jar (a large glass) 2/3 full with crushed ice.

Build drink by adding ingredients. Garnish with remaining lime wheel and two straws.

DATE: April 2001
