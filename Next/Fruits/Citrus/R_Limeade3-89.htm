LIMEADE

Ingredients:
5 West Indian limes
¼ cup white sugar*
2 litres water and crushed ice

Method:
A refreshing drink for those who work in the sun, and those who just sit and watch.

Mix the juice of 5 west Indian limes, with 12 tsp. white sugar. Make up to 2 litres with water and crushed ice.

* ½ cup of light coloured honey can be substituted for sugar.

R. Goebel,
Mossman Digest No.7 April, 1987

DATE: March 1989
