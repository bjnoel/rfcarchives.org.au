FUJIFRUIT WITH COINTREAU ICE

Ingredients:
1/3 cup sugar
½ cup orange juice
3 medium Fujifruit 2
2 cups water
¼ cup Cointreau

Method:
Combine sugar, water and orange juice. Stir over medium heat until sugar has dissolved.

Cool. Strain, add cointreau, pour into freezer tray, freeze.

Peel fujifruit and cut into small segments, arrange into serving glasses or dishes.

Flake ice with fork, spoon on top of fujifruit and serve. Serves 4.

Reprinted from: Persimmon Industry Association Brochure

DATE: July 1996
