FUJIFRUIT HEALTH DRINK

Ingredients:
2 Tbl cottage cheese
3 Tbl plain yoghurt
2 tsp honey
1 medium fujifruit
6 ice-cubes

Method:
Peel and segment fujifruit. Put fujifruit, cottage cheese, honey and yoghurt in blender, blend on medium speed until smooth.

Add ice-cubes, blend further 1 minute.

Serves 2.

Reprinted from: Persimmon Industry Association Brochure

DATE: July 1996
