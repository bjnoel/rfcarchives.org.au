CREAM OF AVOCADO SOUP

Ingredients:
3 avocados
1½ cups chicken broth
1½ cups light cream
1 tsp onion salt
2 tsp lemon juice
1½ teaspoons salt
⅛ teaspoon pepper

Method:
Puree avocados with broth. Combine with cream and seasonings. Chill thoroughly.

Stir in lemon juice. Garnish as desired.

6-8 servings

Avocado Grower's Association of W.A., December, 1983 Newsletter

DATE: February 1984
