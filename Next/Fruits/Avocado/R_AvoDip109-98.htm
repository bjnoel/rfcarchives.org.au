AVOCADO DIP

Ingredients:
1 small onion
1 medium tomato, peeled and diced
1 large ripe avocado, seeded, peeled and diced
250 ml sour cream
20 ml lemon juice
salt and pepper
A few drops of tabasco or chilli sauce

Method:
Blend onion, tomato and avocado; stir in sour cream, salt and pepper, lemon juice and hot sauce to taste.

Serve with crackers.

DATE: November 1998
