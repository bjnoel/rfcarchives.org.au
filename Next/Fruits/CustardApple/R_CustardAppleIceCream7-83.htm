CUSTARD APPLE ICE-CREAM

Ingredients:
1 ripe custard apple
½ cup of thick cream
½ cup of white sugar
1 teaspoon of lemon or other citrus juice.

Method:
Peel the fruit, squeeze the flesh through a fine sieve and measure out 1 cup of juice, add cream, sugar and citrus juice, stirring continuously. Pour the mixture into a refrigerator's ice tray and freeze until solid.

Mildred Kappel, Townsville

DATE: July 1983
