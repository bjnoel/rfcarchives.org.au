BLACK SAPOTE HEALTH CAKE

Ingredients:
4 cups mixed dried fruit
1 cup black sapote
1 Tbl rum or whisky
1 cup orange juice
1½ cups S.R. wholemeal flour
1/3 cup unprocessed bran
1 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp cinnamon
½ cup liquid skim milk
3 egg whites

Method:
Beat egg white till stiff.

Mix remaining ingredients together.

Fold egg whites through the mixture.

Cook in microwave on medium for 14 minutes.
