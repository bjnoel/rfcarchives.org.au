BLACK SAPOTE AND DATE LOAF

Ingredients:
2 black sapotes
1 cup boiling water
2 cups plain flour
¾ cup sugar
1 Tbl butter
1 cup dates
1 tsp bi-carb soda

Method:
Place dates, butter and sugar in a small saucepan, pour in 1 cup boiling water, let it stand to cool a little.

Add black sapote pulp. Add soda and flour mix well.

Pour into a greased loaf tin and cook in a moderate oven for 45 minutes.

Serve cold, sliced, with butter.

Lyn Benson,
Capricornia Branch Newsletter Vol.8 No.5

DATE: November 1991
