BLACK SAPOTE CHEESE CAKE I

Ingredients:
1½ Tbl gelatine
Grated rind of 1 lemon
1½ Tbl lemon juice
½ cup hot water
1/3 cup sugar
8 oz packaged cream cheese
1 carton sour cream
¾ cup sapote pulp
½ cup chopped raisins soaked in 1 Tbl rum.

Method:
In blender, place gelatine, lemon juice and rind and hot water and blend well. Add sugar and cream cheese, blend well. Add fruit pulp and sour cream and blend well. Turn into a bowl and fold in rum raisins.

Turn into baked tart shell or crumb shell and chill well. Decorate with whipped cream.

Joan and Bruce Crees, Mossman N.Q.

DATE: March 1985
