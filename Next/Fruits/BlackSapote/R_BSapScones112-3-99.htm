BLACK SAPOTE SCONES

Ingredients:
1 Tbl butter
½ cup sugar
1 egg
1 cup mashed black sapote
2 - 2¼ cups self raising flour

Method:
Beat butter and sugar. Add egg, then black sapote.

Turn onto a floured board and cut.

Place on a heated, floured tray, brush scones with milk and cook in a very hot oven 225-250°C for 15 - 20 minutes.

DATE: August 1999
