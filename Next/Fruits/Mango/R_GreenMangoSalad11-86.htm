GREEN MANGO SALAD

Ingredients:
4 small green mangoes
¼ cup coconut cream
2 Tbl lemon juice or vinegar
1 small onion, chopped
Salt and pepper to taste

Method:
1. Wash, peel and grate the mangoes.

2. Mix the coconut cream, lemon juice or vinegar in a bowl.

3. Add the grated mango and chopped onion and mix well. Add salt and pepper to taste.

4. Leave to stand for at least 10 minutes before serving. Four servings.

South Pacific Commission

DATE: November 1986
