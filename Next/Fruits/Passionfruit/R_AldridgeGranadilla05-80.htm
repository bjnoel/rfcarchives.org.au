ALDRIDGE GRANADILLA

Ingredients:
1 ripe granadilla
tin of condensed milk (use ¼ to ½)

Method:
Place the seeds of the granadilla plus the white flesh cut into small cubes into a bowl.

Add ¼ to ½ of the condensed milk. Mix well.

Serve chilled and accompanied by fresh pawpaw.

Dawn Gray

DATE: November 1980
