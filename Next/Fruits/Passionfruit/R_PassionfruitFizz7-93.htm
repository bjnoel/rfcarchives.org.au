PASSIONFRUIT FIZZ

Ingredients:
Passionfruit cordial (recipe above)
juice of 3 limes

Method:
4 ways to use the above mixture:
Add a dash of bitters; serve with soda water.

Best in glass of ice-cold water and add pinch of bicarbonate of soda.

Makes a good drink with rum (or rum and pineapple juice - 'Attorney').

Mix with orange juice and a little sugar.

Rene van Raders, Malanda

DATE: July 1993
