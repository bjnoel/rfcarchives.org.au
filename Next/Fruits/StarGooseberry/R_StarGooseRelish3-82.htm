STAR GOOSEBERRY RELISH

Ingredients:
2 Kg star gooseberries (makes about 1 Kg pieces)
For every Kg of pieces, add:
1 large onion, diced
2 tomatoes, chopped
¼ tsp. cayenne pepper
¼ tsp. turmeric
340 g sugar
250 ml white vinegar

Method:
Slice gooseberries from seed in small pieces.

Add remaining ingredients and cook until soft.

Thicken with cornflour.

Pour into hot, sterilised jars and seal.

DATE: March 1982
