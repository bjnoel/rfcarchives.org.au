ROLLINIA MOUSSE

Ingredients:
20 marshmallows
2 Tbl sugar
½ cup water
1 cup rollinia puree
1 cup whipping cream

Method:
Add sugar and marshmallows to water, place over a low heat and stir until a smooth mixture is obtained. Cool.

When mixture is cool, add rollinia puree and let stand in a cool place until partially congealed.

Whip cream and fold in. Pour into mold and freeze.

D. Meneikys
Mossman News

DATE: January 1988
