LYCHEE PARFAIT

Ingredients:
250 g lychees
brandy
pistachio ice cream
whipped cream or whipped topping

Method:
Peel and pit the lychees; marinate them overnight in brandy.

Into five parfait glasses, alternate layers of lychees, pistachio ice cream, and whipped cream or whipped topping until the glasses are filled.

Top with whipped cream or whipped topping and whole, unpeeled lychees.

Tropical Fruit News June 1994

DATE: January 1995
