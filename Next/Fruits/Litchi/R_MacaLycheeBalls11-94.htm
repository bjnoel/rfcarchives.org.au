MACADAMIA NUT AND LYCHEE BALLS

Ingredients:
1 cup chopped macadamia nuts
1 cup chopped sun-dried lychees
1 cup crushed sweet biscuits
1 cup icing sugar
2 Tbl cocoa
125g melted butter
1 Tbl rum
coconut

Method:
Mix all dry ingredients together with sun-dried lychees and macadamia nuts.

Add melted butter and rum and mix well.

Roll into balls, then coat with coconut. Refrigerate.

Katrina Waller
Capricornia Branch Newsletter Vol. 11 No. 4

DATE: November 1994
