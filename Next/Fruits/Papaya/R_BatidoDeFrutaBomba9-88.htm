BATIDO DE FRUTA BOMBA (PAPAYA)

Ingredients:
1 medium papaya (about 1 lb)
Juice of lime
2 Tbl sugar (optional)
½ cup cold milk
1 tsp vanilla (optional)
2/3 cup crushed ice

Method:
Peel the papaya, remove seeds and stringy membrane, and cut into cubes (you should have about 1¼ cups fruit). Puree with the lime and sugar. Add the milk, vanilla and ice and process until smooth and frosty.

Makes 4 servings.

Article by Linda Cicero in the Miami Herald

DATE: September 1986
