PAPAYA SEED DRESSING

Ingredients:
3 Tbl fresh papaya seed (black)
1 tsp dry mustard
2 cups safflower oil
1 small onion (optional)
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup cider vinegar
2 Tbl honey

Method:
Blend dry ingredients and vinegar. Gradually add oil and onion. Add seeds and blend 30 seconds. Good on fruit or salad greens.

Bea Seekins,
Tampa Bay Chapter

DATE: March 1986
