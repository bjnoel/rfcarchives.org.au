PAWPAW AND STRAWBERRY PIE

Ingredients:
1 cup peanuts
3 dried figs
honey, to bind
2 cups firm pawpaw
2 Tbl honey
4 or 5 strawberries
1 sugar banana

Method:
Grind 1 cup peanuts, 3 dried figs, honey to bind. Press into pie plate thinly.

Blend pawpaw, honey, strawberries and banana.

Pour into pie shell and refrigerate until set (2 or 3 hours). Decorate with fresh strawberries and sesame.

Pawpaw will set if a very firm ripe pawpaw is used. If a soft pawpaw is used, gelatine or agar can be added, to assist setting.

DATE: November 1982
