AQUA CHERRY SWEET PICKLE

Ingredients:
1 cup chopped aqua cherry (cut them into 4)
¾ cup raw sugar
Enough vinegar and water (½ + ½) to cover
6 whole cloves
1 tsp broken cinnamon sticks
3-4 chillies (optional)
1 tsp finely-chopped green ginger
½ level tsp salt

Method:
Bring all to boil. Bottle while hot. Keep one month (if possible!)

Len Muller

DATE: January 1981
